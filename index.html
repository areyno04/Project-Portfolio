About Me

With a strong passion for science, I dedicated my focus to understanding the dynamics of the universe. Namely, I attained a BA in physics and got to study the classical and quantum side of how physics works. Through my time studying physics, computational coding was a necessity for solving complex problems otherwise impossible to do on paper (i.e. questions with too much data or those that would take too long to do by hand). Through this experience, I found a love for coding and started learning about the front end side of things on my own time. Now, I discovered that what I really want to do is become a software developer. So, I strive to improve my knowledge in front and back end coding as well as science to thrive in a technology driven society.
